Our Mission

Education is one of the most important foundation stones to a person’s wellbeing, be it financial, spiritual or anything. Our mission as members of Jyoti is to educate and empower needy students who are burdened by the lack of money, for their proper development, to be a ray of hope for them through their dark times of struggles, to enlighten their burdened minds with education.

“Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all.”

-Aristotle

Education

Our main focus in Jyoti is education for the children.Students from classes 3 to 10,come to Jyoti for gaining knowledge and get a proper pathway for there studies.Apart from normal curriculum,in Jyoti we teach basic computer skills to students of secondary section.We have also started the preparation for Navodaya Scholarship exam for students of class 5 to 10.

Medical Health and Care

Medical support is very helpful for children as well as their family.We also provide medical checkup and mental support to our children,so that there is the proper growth of there body as well as of there mind.

Extra Curricular Activities

In Jyoti,apart from education,we try to bring out and develop the hidden talent of a child.We celebrate all the festivals and events of our college with our children of Jyoti,where they perform and showcase there talent.Dance,music,recitation,drama,etc. all of these talents are enhanced in the students by there teachers.

Our History

We conducted our first survey on 09 September 2009 for classes 3 to 8. We distributed forms for admission to this centre from December 2009 onwards. After a short time we received around 200 forms duly filled.We started the classes from 22 March 2010 with a student strength of 200. Initially we started from classes 6 to 8 in our college building itself for which we are very grateful to the college authority.
