A night school for the underpriviliged children living in the outskirts of Jalpaiguri

Events


Survey

This takes place before the intake of the children for Jyoti.Students of our college go to the areas concerned,to take the head count of the children who can join Jyoti,and in this way,local people around the college are enlightened about the existence of a non-profit organisation,which can help them better the lives of there children.


View recent SURVEY photos.

Rakshabandhan

Rakshabandhan is celebrated in Jyoti,our community,to instil the brother-sister love in the hearts of the tiny tots,to teach them the importance of soul brothers & soul sisters,and to respect the dignity of sisters.

View Rakshabandhan photos.

Teachers' Day

Teachers' Day is celebrated as it is celebrated in any school,to teach the small children,the value of teachers in their life.Teachers can be present in any form;in parents,in elder brothers,elder sisters or even in friends of Jyoti.The children are taught to collect knowledge from everywhere they go.

View Teachers' Day photos.

Viswakarma Puja

Viswakarma Puja is an ausipicious puja of our college as we worship the God of Technology,Lord Viswakarma,on this day.The students of Jyoti,perform onstage to the delight of JGEC.They actively participate and enjoy themselves thoroughly.

View Viswakarma Puja photos.

Children's Day

Children's Day is a huge celebration in Jyoti.On this Day,not only Jyoti members,but all college students gather together to celebrate and enjoy with our children.Different performances,interactive sessions in classes,etc happen.In the end we sit together with the children and eat with them a mouth watering lunch.

View Children's Day photos.

Saraswati Puja

Saraswati Puja,the Puja of Mother Saraswati is held in great vigour and fervour here at Jyoti.Jyoti organises its own Pandal,there are offereings to the Mother Goddess and the children also enjoy thoroughly by visiting pandals of all Hostels.

View Saraswati Puja photos.

Sports Day

Sports Day is an event of our college in which the whole college participates with full hearts and energy. The children of Jyoti also actively participate in the sports day that is organised by the JGEC.These tiny tots have their own competition like 100m race,back race,Sack race,etc.

View Sports Day photos.

Jeclat

Jeclat,the socio-cultural Fest of our College,is also the biggest cultural Fest of North Bengal.The children of Jyoti also actively participate in it.The children join in to perform widely much to delight of their own parents and also the students of JGEC.

View Jeclat photos.

Farewell

The Farewell is an emotional cornerstone in the life of every final year batch,so it is a heart warming and emotional event too.The children of Jyoti and the teachers together give anode to the final year students.The Jyoti students sing,dance and entertain the gathered mileau and it becomes an important & soulful event.

View Farewell photos.

Annual Picnic

Picnic is one of the most inclusive events of Jyoti.On this day,the children of Jyoti,from all age groups,are taken on a picnic in mass.The Teachers & co-ordinators of Jyoti,all join in to take the children for a gala time in the Tea Gardens,after which there is a mass lunch for all the students and members of Jyoti.

View Annual Picnic photos.
